E-Summit
The Entrepreneurship Summit is our annual flagship event which is a confluence of visionary students, professionals or anybody who has a passion for leaving a dent in the universe, start his own business or fund a venture and become a trendsetter.
Eureka!
Eureka! is Asias largest B-Model competition which aims at encouraging people from across the globe to flesh out their ideas and form global enterprises that combine technology, vision and business acumen.
National Entrepreneurship Challenge
NEC aims at helping other colleges create an ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs by establishing an E-Cell in their respective institutes.
Startup Support Services
Startup Services Platform provides some of the most necessary services availed by startups at concessional rates as compared to market rates.
FInCoF
Freelancers, Interns & Co-Founders platform connects startups to students who are interested in the kind of work they have to offer & bring their idea to life.
Entrepreneurship and Business Club
Entrepreneurship & Business club is a community where innovative ideas are born & nurtured into successful business ventures. It is the face of E-Cell inside IIT Bombay.
Embracing Autism
Embracing Autism was the Social Initiative taken by Ecell IIT Bombay to promote awareness and knowledge about the spectrum of abilities that grows within these autistic individuals.
EnTech
EnTech is an initiative taken by E-Cell IIT Bombay which envisions to increase the number of technology startups coming out of IIT Bombay. Through this initiative, we plan to connect professors with sound technical background and industry insights to students who are interested in monetizing research projects into successful business ventures.
EnSpace
An official Entrepreneurship Learning Platform by E-Cell, IIT Bombay.
